Moving Maui

The Perfect Thrill

This is the trip that will thrill you but not scare you. The best family adventure along with everyone else. Lots of great scenery, just enough splashes, and nothing but smiles by the end. You will need to arrive at 7 am to be outfitted and instructed for the ride. After pushing off you will encounter some quick water and gentle rapids for the first 30 min. With that experience and the next 30 min of calm waters you will prep and be ready for the next 60 minutes of bigger but do able rapids. Which then comes to the slower calmer waters that you can hang loss and enjoy the scenery. This is a great time to jump in and get wet if you aren't already. Maybe declare a water war with your neighboring rafters. To finish off this great trip there is a last bit of rapids with our professional photographers ready to snap that keep sake the help you remember you rafted the Moving Maui!
