Moving Maui

The Perfect Thrill

This is the trip that will thrill you but not scare you, for ages 6+. The best family adventure along with everyone else. Lots of great scenery, just enough splashes, and nothing but smiles by the end. As we set off the river will gently rolls you along so you can get used to working together as a team. Then the fun begins with our Moving Maui rapid set. They will get you excited for more. There is a lot to look out through out the ride when you get a chance.

Tetrarch Thrill

Adventure around Every Bend

This trip will be all you talk about for quite some time, for ages 13+. This is a half day adventure that puts you over some of the most legendary rapids the Wyoming River has to offer. With our trained and experienced guides, they know how to get you in the action and get you safely through. This eight-mile stretch has a few starter rapids to get you trained and ready to hit the big ones. You will work together as a raft team to navigate through, feeling a sense of unity of braving and riding these amazing rapids.

Scenic Ride

Gentle Sight Seeing Wonder

This trip is known for its photography moments, for ages 6+. Scenery landscape enthusiasts have come from all around the world to capture the amazing landscape and wildlife that are seen on this ride. The gentle flow of the water and amazement of what God has created has made this stretch of the river a treasure the Lutz Rafting Co. Often there will be wildlife spotted close to the river amongst the trees and foulage. There is an open meadow that in the spring we call in the rafters delight, having spotted many of baby deer frolicking.
