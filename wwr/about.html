History

The history of the Lutz Rafting Co dates back to 1962. It is a family owned company running the river for over 60 years now. There has been a lot of time on this river. We have the best location and trips available, they are time tested and approved. The best campgrounds and most amazing rapids at all levels. And the staff are experienced and trained. We are excited to jump into this great adventure.
